The Methodology

How we go from your raw material to a working system. Four phases, no hand-waving.

01

Analyze

We read everything — books, transcripts, curricula, internal docs — and map the core ideas, sequences, and gaps.

02

Structure

We turn your raw content into organized modules, learning sequences, and a knowledge base the AI can draw from.

03

Build

We design and build the platform, set up the learning paths, and train the AI on your content.

04

Improve

We watch how people actually use the system — where they drop off, what they ask the AI, which paths work — and we refine it.
