About

Movemental started with a problem: important bodies of work were stuck in books and talks, with no way to become living systems.

Alan Hirsch spent decades studying how movements actually grow — not the marketing version, but the structural patterns beneath apostolic and missional communities. That research became the foundation for Movemental.

Now we take that same structural thinking and apply it: turning a leader's body of work into platforms, pathways, courses, and AI layers that actually function as systems — not just collections of content.

"Structure is the skeleton; movement is the soul."

— Alan Hirsch

How the process works.

Movemental is a system-building process. We take what a leader has already created — the books, frameworks, and ideas — and give it structural form: organized, navigable, and built to work without them in the room.
