The Overview

The Digital Curator Institutional Press provides the foundational texts and curriculum for this intensive study. All sessions are recorded for the private archive.

Institutional leadership is no longer about managing change—it is about curating the transformation of the very fabric of knowledge and decision-making.

This 8-week intensive is designed for leaders who view AI not as a tool, but as a new architectural layer for their organizations. We move beyond "prompt engineering" into the realm of **Institutional Logic Transformation**.

Participants will engage with faculty from the intersections of Computer Science, Ethics, and Organizational Design to develop a bespoke transformation blueprint for their respective entities.
